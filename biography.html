Where am I from?

Chicago

I was born in the city of Chicago on July 17th, 1994. I lived there for about 5 years before moving to Sammamish where I've lived in for the majority of my life. I love visiting Chicago regularly because my entire mother's side of the family uprooted from China and moved there.

Sammamish

I moved to Washington State when I was around 5 years old. I lived in Renton for 1 year before settling down in Sammamish to the present day. I really enjoy the weather in the Seattle Area where it's not too hot, not too cold. I for one welcome the balance of rain, sunshine, and occasional snowfall.
